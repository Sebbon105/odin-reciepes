Lemon Pasta Recipe

Tasty, Creamy lemon Pasta

This nice and easy recipe for pasta, is something you dont want to miss!

    Ingridients

  • Pasta
  • Lemon
  • Basil
  • Chili flakes
  • Olive oil
  • Garlic
  • Salt and pepper

    FORWARD STEPS

  1. Cut the garlic and fry it on the pan with chili flakes in a lot of olive oil
  2. Warm some water and boil the pasta
  3. When the pasta is done, add a little pasta-water to the pan, and all of the pasta
  4. Add lemon juice, basil, salt and pepper for taste
  5. Enjoy!
