Halloumi Burger (veg)

Nice and simple halloumi burgers

This nice halloumi burger will make you want simple veggie-burgers more often!

Ingridients

  • Burger bread
  • Hallumi Cheese
  • Mayonaise
  • Lettuce
  • Tomatos
  • salt
  • Pepper

FORWAD STEPS

  1. Cut the tomatoes and lettuce in nice slices
  2. Cut the halloumi in slices and put it on the grill until it looks nice a golden-brown
  3. Cut the bread and put it on the grill
  4. Time to assemble the burger
  5. Spread mayo on both halfs, add lettuce, halloumi and tomatos (in that order) - and finish off with salt and pepper.
  6. Enjoy!
