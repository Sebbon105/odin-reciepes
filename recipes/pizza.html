Pizza Dough Recipe

Neapolitan Pizza

This recipe is the best one ever for simple but VERY good neapolitan pizza at home

    Ingridients for the "Pre-dough"

  • 100g water
  • 5g dry yeast
  • 5g honey
  • 100g tipo 00 flour

    Ingridients for the dough (6 pizzas)

  • all of the pre-dough
  • 700g water
  • 1000g flour
  • 15g salt

STEP FOWARD

  1. Make the pre-dough by adding 100g of water and 5g dry yeats to a bowl and mix it around.
  2. Add 5g og honey/sugar to help the yeast
  3. Mix the 100g of flour in the water-mix and put it in the fridge for 6-16 hours
  4. Take the pre-dough out the fridge and add it to a bowl
  5. Add 700g of water, 1000g of flour, 15g of salt to the pre-dough and mix well
  6. When the dough is mixed all together, leave it on the counter for 10-15 min, then shape it to a round ball
  7. After 15 min, fold the dough 3-5 times and then leave it on the counter, covered, for 1 hour
  8. After 1 hour you cut the dough into 6 pieces (dougballs) and fold them like small new balls to rest again for 1 hour
  9. Congrats! - your nice and round pizza balls, filled with air is now ready to use
  10. Enjoy! ;D
